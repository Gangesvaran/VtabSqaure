IT Staffing

Resource On-boarding

Resource on-boarding is process of introducing a new employee into the organization and its culture. On-boarding activities involve the tools and information that are needed to interact with the team right after they’ve signed their offer letter. On-boarding may be a crucial neighborhood of helping employees understand their new position and job requirements. Employees will feel confident and competent when the on-boarding process is complete.

Contract hiring

Contract staffing means an employee should sign and comply with the terms of an employer before working. Contractual work is usually for a specified amount of time and ends upon completion of a project or assignment. The payment will be on fixed terms with some provision for increment (usually predetermined). The contract hire is not given statutory benefits.
